ABOUT ME

My name is Andre Dantas and I'm a Game Programmer living in Montreal, Canada.

Lover of new technologies, programming became to me the outlet to exploring new visual and interactive applications. Through this outlet I feel motivated and eager to learn new skills every day.

It all started as a hobby, but over the last few years of doing this on my free time, I decided that it was time to actively seek a career in this domain.

I'm used to working in different environments, from small studios to big companies, and I love tackling new challenges, no matter their size.
